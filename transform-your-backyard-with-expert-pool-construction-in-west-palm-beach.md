## **Understanding the Cost of New Swimming Pool Construction**

### **Inground Swimming Pool Cost in West Palm Beach**

One of the most common questions we receive is, “How much does it cost to
build a pool in West Palm Beach?” The **inground swimming pool cost** varies
depending on the size, type, and features selected. On average, **custom
concrete pools** can range from $50,000 to $150,000 or more.

### **Factors Influencing Pool Construction Costs**

  * **Size of the pool:** Smaller pools or **small inground pools** are generally less expensive.
  * **Design complexity:** Unique shapes, water features, or spa integrations increase costs.
  * **Materials:** Concrete pools tend to be more durable but may require larger investments.
  * **Additional features:** Custom tile work, stone accents, water features, and automation systems add to the overall price.
