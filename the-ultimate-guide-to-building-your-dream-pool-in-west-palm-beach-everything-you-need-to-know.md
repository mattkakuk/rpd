##  **Construction Process: From Start to Finish**

###  **Site Preparation and Excavation**

Preparation involves clearing the site, grading, and excavation. This phase is
crucial to ensure proper drainage and stability, especially considering
Florida's sandy soil.

###  **Foundation and Forming**

Next, the formwork is created to shape your pool based on your design
specifications. Reinforcements like steel bars are embedded for strength.

###  **Pouring and Finishing Concrete Pools**

For **concrete pools** , high-quality gunite or shotcrete is poured, then
finished with plaster, tile, or pebble surfaces.

###  **Installing Equipment and Water Features**

Once the shell cures, plumbing, filtration, lighting, and additional features
are installed by experienced **swimming pool contractors**.

###  **Final Inspection and Filling**

After passing inspections, your pool is filled with water, treated, and ready
for use.
